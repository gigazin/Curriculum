Welcome! I'm Danilo Silva, a B.Sc Computer Science Student. 

                Born in 2001 at Recife, Pernambuco, I'm a brazilian programmer and designer. I had my first contacts with a computer 
 when I was still a child, around 2005. Since the very first time I got to use a computer I started wondering myself "how it works?". 
 But, as the time passed by I ended up forgetting about it as I was too young to search for answers.

                As I grew up, this question came back to my mind, levering my interest for computers and the engineering behind them. 
 So I spent my entire high school period studying about computers, their history, programming and design as well, which was my 
 main working goal back then. It was, undoubtedly, the period where I learned the most in my life.

But, it was already time to join college... 

                After graduating from high school in 2019 I started studying to join a Computer Science college. The target 
 was joining a public university (Federal University of Pernambuco), but unfortunately my grade was too low. But, as 
 we brazilians like to say - I'm brazilian, and I never give up.

                After failing my first attempt to join college in a public university, I tried once again, but a private one this 
 time around, and I did it! My second option was the Catholic University of Pernambuco, and I joined it by the second 
 half of 2020, fortunately with a full scholarship as I couldn't afford the monthly payment of the course.

                And now I'm currently studying Computer Science, already at the 5th semester of college and very close to 
 reach my dream of graduating, with expectations to do it in December 2024. This dream has a huge meaning to me, 
 as it was my father's dream to see his son graduating, but unfortunately he passed away when I was halfway through 
 the course.

And I developed some skills...
